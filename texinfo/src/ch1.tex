    A simple minimalistic text in chapter 1. It will be processed by \emph{latex2texi}.

    \section{Format Latex for Texi}

    A simple converter will convert \LaTeX\ chapters to \texttt{texi} chapters. 
    It will strip down the \underline{images}.

    \section{Mathematics}

    The inline formulae are \textbf{valid} for texi \(j=j_0 \cdot \left(1-\exp \frac{x}{x_0}\right)\) and
    the notation with the dollar \textit{sign} is not supportted $\alpha _{gg}$.
    
    as well as multine.

    \section{Figures}
    Figures are also supported. The \texttt{includegraphics} command 
    will be extracted and transformed to the \texttt{texi} equivalent command. 
    
    \begin{figure}
        \centering
        \includegraphics[width=0.5\textwidth]{image.pdf}
        \caption{caption only on one line.}
        \label{fig_caption}
    \end{figure}
